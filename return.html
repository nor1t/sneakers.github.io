Return Policy



At .sneakers, we strive to provide our customers with high-quality sneakers that meet and exceed their expectations. However, we understand that there may be instances where you need to return or exchange your purchase. Our return policy is designed to be fair, transparent, and hassle-free.
30-Day Return Policy: We offer a 30-day return policy from the date of purchase. If, for any reason, you are not completely satisfied with your sneakers, you may return them within 30 days for a refund or exchange.
Conditions for Returns: 1.Unused and Unworn: Sneakers must be in their original, unused, and unworn condition. Please ensure that all tags, labels, and packaging are intact. 2.Proof of Purchase: A valid proof of purchase, such as the order confirmation email or receipt, is required for all returns. 3.Return Authorization: Prior to returning your sneakers, please contact our customer service team at customer-service@.sneakers.com to obtain a Return Authorization (RA) number. This number must be clearly marked on the outside of the return package.
Exclusions: The following items are not eligible for return:
- Sneakers that show signs of wear or use beyond the original condition.
- Sneakers without their original packaging or with damaged packaging.
- Items purchased from unauthorized sellers.
Refund Process: Once we receive your returned sneakers and inspect them for compliance with our return policy, we will process your refund or exchange. Refunds will be issued to the original method of payment. Please allow 5 business days for the refund to reflect in your account.
Return Shipping: Customers are responsible for the cost of return shipping, unless the return is due to a manufacturing defect or an error on our part. We recommend using a trackable shipping method to ensure the safe return of your sneakers.
Damaged or Defective Items: If you receive damaged or defective sneakers, please contact our customer service team within 7 days of receiving your order. We will provide instructions for the return or exchange of the defective item. At .sneakers, we are committed to ensuring your satisfaction with every purchase. If you have any questions or concerns regarding our return policy, please contact our customer service team at customer-service@.sneakers.com. Thank you for choosing .sneakers for your sneaker needs!
